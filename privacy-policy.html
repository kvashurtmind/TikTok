Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2025

Vibes Videos ("Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Service, which enables automated content posting to TikTok from Telegram channels.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • TikTok Access Tokens: Obtained via OAuth 2.0 to post content on your behalf.
  • Content: Videos and descriptions provided through Telegram channels for posting to TikTok.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Uploading and publishing content to your TikTok account via the TikTok API.

We do not share your information with third parties, except as required to interact with the TikTok API.

3. Storage and Security

We store:

  • Access tokens in an encrypted format until they expire.
  • Content temporarily for processing and posting.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

4. Your Rights

You may:

  • Revoke access to your TikTok account through TikTok’s settings.
  • Request the deletion of your data by contacting us at kvashurtmind@gmail.com.

5. Compliance with Laws

We comply with applicable data protection laws. If you have any concerns about your privacy, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy
